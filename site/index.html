How to read the map

The filled field is each model's initialized 300-hPa wind speed. Black labeled contours are initialized 300-hPa geopotential heights in decameters. Radiosonde dots are colored and sized by absolute wind-speed error; select a dot for observed and modeled winds, heights, and detailed errors.

Initialization fit is not independent forecast skill; radiosonde observations may have been assimilated by the models. View source on GitHub
